Continuing our adventures through Europe!

2016 is almost over and we have much to be thankful for! After almost two years in the UK now behind us, we feel right at home.

The kids are adjusted and happy at their schools. Caleb is active in youth group, helped his team get 2nd place in lacrosse, and has joined a school basketball team. No matter the weather, Tyler loves playing outside in the forest behind our house, and has also joined school clubs like Japanese art, cookery, and "forest school" (outdoor learning). Tayte loves school, has a lot of friends, and has also joined after-school clubs like music, computing, and Lego.

Tim is chugging along at his job in London and exploring it's many sights whenever he gets the chance. Chesney's schedule is full with two jobs and is an active member in a women's organization.

We love being able to explore destinations in Europe on our holidays, and Tim enjoys documenting it all in his photos! We've included some of the highlights of our travels in 2016 here (Click on a photo for more details).

Thank you to the many friends and family to visited us in the past year! May 2017 even better!
